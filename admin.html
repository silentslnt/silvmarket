About Broadcast

Broadcasts push a notification directly into every registered user's notification feed. They'll see it next time they open their account page.

Use sparingly — every user gets this regardless of what game they play.

Good uses
• Site-wide sales or limited time events
• New game sections added to the shop
• Important order or delivery updates
