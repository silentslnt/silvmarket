Order Steps
1
Add Items to Your Cart
Browse through our game sections and add everything you want to your cart. You can mix items from multiple games in a single order.
2
Proceed to Checkout
Open your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". Review your items and quantities before moving forward.
3
Enter Roblox Username & Link Discord
Enter your Roblox username accurately — this is how staff will deliver your items. Link your Discord account so your ticket is created for the right account.
4
Apply Promo Code
If you have a promo code, enter it in the discount field to apply your discount. Minimum order of $10 required for promo codes.
5
Choose Payment Method
Select your preferred payment method — Stripe (credit/debit card) or crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL, USDT, LTC). Complete the payment through the secure checkout.
6
Ticket Created on Discord
After payment, a private support ticket is automatically created in our Discord server for your linked account. Make sure you've joined the server.
7
Receive Your Items
Delivery staff will reach out through your ticket and deliver your items in-game via Roblox. Follow their instructions and you're all set.
Secure Payments
All transactions processed through Stripe or crypto — fully encrypted.
